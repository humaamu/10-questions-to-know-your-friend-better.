1. What's something you've always wanted to try but haven't had the chance to yet?

This question taps into your friend’s dreams or aspirations. It gives them a chance to share something they’ve thought about but haven’t acted on yet, whether it's a new hobby, adventure, or personal goal.

2. If you could have dinner with any three people, living or dead, who would they be?

This one reveals who they admire or find interesting. Whether it’s historical figures, family members, or even fictional characters, this question helps you understand their values, interests, and curiosity.

3. What's a memory that always makes you smile when you think about it?

This one encourages a positive, nostalgic trip down memory lane. It’s a lighthearted question that helps your friend reflect on a happy moment, which can deepen your bond when you both share positive stories.

4.If you could live anywhere in the world for a year, where would it be and why?

This is an intriguing way to dive into your friend's sense of adventure or longing. It can reflect their interests—whether they crave nature, culture, or city life—and can lead to discussions about travel and exploring the unknown.

5.What's the best advice you've ever received?

This question lets your friend share wisdom or life lessons they've picked up over the years. It can lead to deeper conversations about values, guiding principles, and experiences that shaped their perspective.

6.If your life was a movie, what would the title be?

A fun and creative question! It encourages your friend to think about their life in a different light, possibly revealing their sense of humor, their view of themselves, or how they interpret their personal journey.

7.What's one thing you would change about the world if you could?

This is a deeper question that invites your friend to reflect on the world around them and consider societal or global issues they feel passionate about. It shows what matters to them and can spark a meaningful conversation about change and activism.

8.If you won the lottery tomorrow, what’s the first thing you’d do?

This question is about dreams and priorities. It can reveal a lot about your friend’s true desires—whether they’re into travel, philanthropy, or material things. It’s a fun, speculative question that brings out their ideal scenario.

9.What’s a hobby or skill you’ve always wanted to pick up but never did?

This taps into any unfulfilled interests or things they wish they’d explored more. It could be a hobby they think is too difficult or something they’ve been too busy to try. It can also lead to conversations about regrets or things they’re still curious about.

10. If you could instantly master any new skill or talent, what would it be?

This is another "what-if" question, but it focuses on self-improvement or personal growth. Your friend might talk about skills that could enhance their life, such as learning a new language, playing an instrument, or even mastering something practical.
